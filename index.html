Women’s Health · Family Health · Nutrition · Health Equity

Research that supports women, parents, children, and families.

The Dr. Ortiz Research Lab studies how family-centered care, nutrition, stress, and social context shape health across generations. Our work aims to advance equitable, community-informed approaches to clinical care and public health.
