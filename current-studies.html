Study 01

Exploring the Association Between the Caregiver-Child Relationship and Cardiovascular Health

This study explores how caregiver-child relationships, stress, lifestyle habits, and heart health may be connected. The project supports a better understanding of cardiovascular health within family and caregiving contexts.

Perspectives on a Family-Centered Approach to Medically Tailored Meals

This study focuses on experiences with medically tailored meals and how these programs may support families. The goal is to better understand participant perspectives and inform future family-centered nutrition support.

DDS Pre-screen

Real-Time Meal Intake and Context Tracking Using EMA
