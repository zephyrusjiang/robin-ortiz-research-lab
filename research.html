Research Areas

Our lab explores how health is shaped by family relationships, nutrition, stress, clinical care, and social context.

Studying pregnancy-related health, maternal wellbeing, and clinical care experiences.

Family-Centered Care

Understanding how caregivers, children, and families experience health interventions together.

Nutrition & Meal Support

Exploring medically tailored meals, dietary context, and nutrition support for families.

Cardiovascular Health

Measuring and improving cardiovascular health among parents and families.
