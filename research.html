Research

Intergenerational and relational approaches to cardiovascular health.

Dr. Ortiz’s research centers on cardiovascular health, caregiver-child dyads, structural adversity, lifecourse health, and neuro-endocrine mechanisms.

Research Summary

Dr. Ortiz is the Principal Investigator and founder of the Caregiver Heart Health research clinic and program. Her work aims to re-imagine healthcare through an intergenerational and relational health framework.

The lab’s work explores how caregiver-child relationships and family-level contexts can help shape cardiovascular health trajectories across the lifecourse.
